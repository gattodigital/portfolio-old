About Me

Who I Am

Hello World! I am Andres Ramirez, a Web Designer and Front End Developer based out of Saint Louis, Missouri. Passionate about computers and the web since I was 10 years old, I have a clear understanding of the importance of a clean and modern website for success in the new digital world we live in. My mission is to reach out to small businesses, artists and entrepreneurs to help them succeed by providing my services of Web Design, Online Marketing and E-Commerce.

My web career started back in 2004, when I casually began selling bargain electronics on eBay, and then grew that hobby to a multi-national business grossing over $1M in total sales in a span of 6 years. In 2011 I decided to take my career to the next level, and enrolled in the Miami Arts Institute to study Web Design and Interactive Media. Nowadays I continue to study on my own account through online tutorials, and I practice the trade as a Freelancer while searching for a permanent position within a strong successful company.

Services

I don't just build websites... I build your business!

Web Design

I specialize in creating unique websites tailored to help your business succeed. My websites are fully responsive and compatible with all screen sizes. Clients have the option of choosing a template based design, or having a fully customized website developed with their own concepts and ideas.

Online Marketing

In order to give your website the maximum exposure, I offer SEO (Search Engine Optimization), PPC (pay-per-click) campaigns, social media, and blogging services. These services will bring you larger amounts of targeted traffic, which translates to business growth!

E-Commerce

Why sell only locally, when you could sell globally? I can develop an attractive e-commerce website for you with product galleries, shopping cart, and secure payment integration. Along with your own e-commerce website, I also recommend and assist in setting up stores with other sales channels such as eBay and Amazon.

Maintenance & Support

Your website is up and running, but quickly getting outdated. Whether you want to make a few tweaks or adjustments, add some content, or redesign the whole site... I'm here to help! My job does not end when the the final product is delivered. I want to stay for the long term and help your business grow through the internet!
